# US Construction Spending Analysis (Python)

[View Interactive Report](https://htmlpreview.github.io/?https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Tommy-Nguyen-Stonera/python-construction-spending-analysis/main/report.html)

14 years of monthly US construction spending data (2002-2016) analysed with Python and Pandas. Covers the pre-GFC boom, the crash, and the long recovery across residential, commercial, manufacturing, and public sectors.

## Key Findings

- Total spending grew around 30%, but the path included a massive GFC collapse and slow recovery
- Residential spending crashed hardest during GFC and was slowest to recover
- Private spending is far more volatile than public spending through economic cycles
- Manufacturing and healthcare were the standout growth sectors
- Seasonal patterns in national data are weaker than expected

## Tools

Python 3.12, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
